Crocheted and Knitted Works

Crocheted Flowers

Crocheted tulips following da Mira’s tutorial on Youtube and crocheted lily of the valley flowers were made following Jovelyn B.’s tutorial. The glasswares were upcycled from unwanted glassware in biology labs at Lily, Purdue’s Hall of Life Sciences.

Bonus: minature version of the flowers above. The small tulips were made by reducing the number of rows in the flower.

Crocheted Dragon Scale Dice Pouches

These little drawstring pouches to hold dice were made as gifts for my friends who played DnD. The pattern for the pouch can be found on Ellie Lenz’s Youtube Channel. The size of the pouch from the tutorial has been reduced (by lowering the number of rows in the bottom) to be more suitable for holding dice.

Ceramics

Planters

The planter below was hand-built, a ceramics technique that does not involve the pottery wheel, to give it a more organic look. Then it was painted using underglaze, which provided better control over color placement, before the piece was fired with clear glaze to seal all the colors in. Unfortunately, while inside the kiln, glaze from a piece above dripped onto the planter.

This second planter was made using a pottery wheel, hence its sides are straighter. In addition, the planter was fired with glaze instead of underglaze.
